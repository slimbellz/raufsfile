Our Company

Triumph Partner Multiservices Limited (TPML) was set up in 2012 by a group of Nigerian professionals in the Pension Industry who are desirous of creating a niche for its self by promoting value proposition philosophy that creates a value added positive change and paradigm shift in the industry operation. The main thrust of the company is to carry out corporate re-engineering and consultancy services that guarantees optimal returns to all the stakeholders.

Vision

To be the choice partner of every stakeholder seeking a turnaround in enterprise value by connecting dreams to reality.

Mission

To turn around nonperforming firms to industry leaders by putting in place best case scenario models and practice in processes, technology, business development and corporate governance that become a culture.

Award

Within the short period of our managing the PFA and implementing the turnaround exercise, the National Pension Commission issued a Commendation Letter to the PFA for compliance and promptness in meeting regulatory deadlines.
